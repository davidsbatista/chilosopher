Chilosopher

Most of these songs were just guitar jams, riffs and chord progressions which I had already had in my mind for a while, and others that I just recorded spontaneously. They were very unprofessional recorded, some including background noise but I like them like that, it was my first recording experiment.

"The Beginning" - something that came up, spontaneously, one of the riffs I already had in my head, the melody on top was recorded on the fly.

"Water" - the chord progression I already had on my mind for a while, just slow it down, and the melody was improvised and recorded on-the-fly.

"Balance" - balancing between over-creating and enjoying the simplicity of things, and getting hooked on a very cool "too-much-delay-and-flanger" effect.
